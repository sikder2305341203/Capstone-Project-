The Impact of High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) on Metabolic Rate

Abstract: An extensive study on the human metabolic response to intense, short-duration exercises. Results show that HIIT sessions lasting just 20 minutes can elevate resting Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) for up to 24 hours post-workout, accelerating fat loss without muscle degradation.

Abstract: Exploring the correlation between sleep quality and muscle recovery. The research demonstrates that getting less than 6 hours of sleep reduces muscle protein synthesis by 18% and increases cortisol levels, which actively hinders weight management goals.
